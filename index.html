throughout this web installation, you are encountering a series of works orbiting around a transient glitch phenomenon within geovisualization software. it is the documentation of an artistic practice in isolation, a meditative obsession unfolding between 2020 and 2022.

a glitch is proud to be wrong. failing means refusing to take a system for granted. to counter habits, and to acknowledge difference.
salty glitches, stay a little longer, bug and orbital mirage are collections of code-born unintentions. they allow one to slip below the surface of seamless mapping technologies. to question their smoothness, to sense their fragility. to uncover new forms of being with media.
my practice is grounded in erring. zooming in and out. losing orientation. a state of trance. eventually, one finds oneself. finding oneself with a glitch once more. screenshotting as archiving, screen recording and continuous observation become a gesture of care ‚Äì in preserving a moment in awe.

salty glitches salty glitches

six archival prints of approx. 400 screenshots, qr-codes

salty glitches is a counter-mapping project that troubles the notion of truth in digital media. its colorful anomalies challenge mapping technologies‚Äô claim to represent objective worldviews ‚Äì while drawing attention to zones of ecological disaster and social injustice.

the qr-code links to google maps, where, by 2022, most of these glitches quietly disappeared through bugfixing.

each color fragment was emblematically sitting nearby the atacama desert and the jujuy region, both reservoirs of the precious mineral lithium.

viewing these images on a smartphone or laptop potentially means viewing them through a device that is materially connected to these very places.

While meditation emerges as a popular coping strategy for inner healing and self-care in our digitized everyday lives, technologies heal too. Bugs are being fixed. Hopeful uncertainties vanish.

The 16-channel video installation 'stay a little longer, bug' follows the vivid existence of random digital phenomena. It searches for the qualities and visual ecstasies of unintended code issues. By slowly getting closer, it lets the bugs speak, show their inner being, and breathe.

orbital mirage extends the practice of observation into the planetary scale. it transforms a series of satellite anomalies into a public archive of digital accidents ‚Äî traces of invisible computation, drifting in orbit and stored within the global cloud. every marker on this map points to a site where the algorithm faltered, revealing the poetic instability of the technologies that surround us.
