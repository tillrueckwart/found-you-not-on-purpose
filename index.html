While meditation emerges as a popular coping strategy for inner healing and self-care in our digitized everyday lives, technologies heal too. Bugs are being fixed. Hopeful uncertainties vanish.

The 16-channel video installation 'stay a little longer, bug' follows the vivid existence of random digital phenomena. It searches for the qualities and visual ecstasies of unintended code issues. By slowly getting closer, it lets the bugs speak, show their inner being, and breathe.
